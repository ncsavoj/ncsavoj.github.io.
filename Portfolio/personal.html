Filling My Freetime

At home

My wife and I purchased an old 1.5 story "fixer-upper" in Union Grove when we got married.
We currently have no children so we decided to fill our house with pets. A lot of pets.
Our most recent addition is an 8 week old lab-Australian shepherd mix named Romeo.

When we are not taking care of the animals
we like to spend a lot of our free time outdoors.
We regularly take the canoe out on fishing tips
and in the fall you can usually find us perched
in a tree or sitting in a ground blind watching for deer.
